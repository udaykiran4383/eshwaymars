Bioscience Subsystem

Preliminary tests for moisture, temperature, and methane content are performed using integrated sensors.
Microscopy technique is executed on rover to observe presence of bacteria using gram staining method, further quantifying the observations using image processing.
In-situ visible and UV range spectrometry is used to identify presence of biomolecules like proteins and ATP in the sample.
New techniques such as microfluidics are being explored as potential on-rover bio-assay methods.
